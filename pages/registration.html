Concession in Registration Fees for Academic Development

The Organizing Committee members of ISACC 2025 are committed to promoting international academic exchange and global scholarly participation. To encourage broader involvement, reduced registration fees have been introduced specifically for attendees from abroad who may benefit from it. This initiative aims to enhance accessibility for researchers, students, and professionals from the global academic community.

For more information on the reduced fee structure, please email: isacc.cseaus@gmail.com
